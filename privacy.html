Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

1. Information Collection

Brofolio is designed with a privacy-first approach for real estate professionals. The app does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

To support application functionality, subscription status management, and service reliability, Brofolio generates a random anonymous user identifier (UUID). This identifier is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to personally identify you.

Users may voluntarily enter property information, portfolio records, client contact details, and property search preferences into the application. These records are created and managed solely by the user for business purposes.

2. How the App Works

Brofolio is a portfolio management application designed for real estate agents and property professionals. The app allows users to:

  • Create and manage property portfolios
  • Store property details such as land, field, apartment, commercial property, and other real estate characteristics
  • Create and organize contact lists
  • Record customer property preferences and requirements
  • Match available portfolios with customer search criteria
  • Import and export portfolio data using CSV files

Brofolio does not use user data for advertising, profiling, behavioral analysis, or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Information entered into Brofolio may be stored using secure backend infrastructure provided by Supabase.

Stored data may include:

  • Anonymous user identifier (UUID)
  • Property portfolio records created by the user
  • Customer and contact records entered by the user
  • Property search criteria and matching information
  • Subscription-related status information

This information is stored solely to provide the core functionality of the application and is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

CSV import and export files are generated and managed by the user. Brofolio does not use imported or exported files for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Brofolio uses a limited number of third-party services strictly necessary for application functionality:

  • Supabase – secure backend database and data storage infrastructure
  • RevenueCat – subscription management and purchase validation
  • Apple In-App Purchases – payment processing for subscriptions and premium features

Brofolio does not use third-party advertising networks, behavioral tracking services, marketing SDKs, or analytics platforms.

5. Payments and Subscription Data

All purchases and subscription transactions are processed securely through Apple In-App Purchases.

Subscription status and entitlement management are handled through RevenueCat.

Brofolio does not collect, process, or store payment card information, banking details, or billing information.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational security measures are implemented to protect data stored within the application.

Data transmission and storage rely on industry-standard security practices provided by trusted infrastructure partners.

While no system can guarantee absolute security, Brofolio is designed to minimize data collection and reduce privacy risks wherever possible.

7. User Rights

Users maintain control over the information they enter into Brofolio.

Users may modify, export, or remove portfolio records, customer records, and other business information managed within the application.

If you have questions regarding data associated with your anonymous user identifier or subscription status, you may contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Brofolio is intended for use by real estate professionals and business users. The application is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Brofolio does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International Data Processing

Depending on your location, information stored through Supabase and related infrastructure providers may be processed on servers located in different countries.

Appropriate safeguards are implemented by service providers to protect stored information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect application improvements, legal requirements, or operational changes.

Continued use of Brofolio after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
